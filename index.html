Remoku: Getting Started Remoku is a web app for Roku Remote Control.

To get started, you can search for Rokus if you enter your network address range* below, then click the Scan button to look for Roku devices. Or, you can manually add the address of a Roku.

If the scan doesn't find any Rokus, please look for help at help.remoku.tv.

* For example: 192.168.1.X. These numbers can be found within the Network Control Panel or Wifi Settings of most devices, and/or in your router manual.
