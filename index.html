Remoku: Getting Started Remoku is a web app for Roku Remote Control. To begin, Remoku needs to know the address of your Roku(s).

You can search for Rokus if you enter your network address range* below, then click the Scan button to look for Roku devices or manually add the address of a Roku.

If the scan doesn't find any Rokus, look for help at help.remoku.tv.

* Eg. 192.168.1.X, this can be found in the Network Control Panel or Wifi Settings areas of most devices.
